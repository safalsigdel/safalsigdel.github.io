Mercedes-Benz automotive group and Rivian have a new joint venture to build commercial electric vans for both automakers. The companies announced today that they signed a Memorandum of Understanding to start a strategic partnership that will build the new vans on an existing Mercedes-Benz site in Central / Eastern Europe.
